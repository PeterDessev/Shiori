Shiori（栞） — learn Japanese, and other languages, by reading. Dual-licensed MIT or Apache-2.0.

Shiori ships no dictionary data; it downloads its Japanese reference data on first run, and language packs on demand. JMdict © EDRDG (EDRDG licence), KANJIDIC2 © EDRDG (CC BY-SA 4.0), KanjiVG © Ulrich Apel (CC BY-SA 3.0). Books from Aozora Bunko and Japanese Wikisource. Packs built from Wiktionary use kaikki.org’s Wiktextract data (CC BY-SA 4.0 & GFDL) and hermitdave’s FrequencyWords lists (CC BY-SA 4.0); each installed pack states its own license in the app. Full attributions live in the app’s About section.
